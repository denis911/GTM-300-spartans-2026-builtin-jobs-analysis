# GTM Landing Page Vision

## Goal
A high-fidelity report landing page that presents the findings of the 2026 GTM Engineering Job Market Analysis. It should feel authoritative, premium, and data-dense.

## Core Message
The GTM Engineering role is maturing. We analyzed 310 curated roles across 5 global hubs to reveal the compensation, tools, and technical requirements defining the field in 2026.

## Section Outline
1. **Hero**: Title, subtle background animation, "March 2026 Edition" badge.
2. **The Pulse (Stats Ribbon)**: 310 Roles Analyzed, 5 Global Hubs, $218k Median Salary.
3. **The Leaders (Section 1)**: Top 10 Companies Hiring GTM Engineers (High-density table).
4. **Role Diversity (Section 2)**: Distribution of Technical GTM vs. Pure Tech roles (Styled table).
5. **The Toolkit (Section 5)**: Most demanded tools (Clay, n8n, Salesforce, etc.) with a clean chart.
6. **Compensation (Section 3)**: Salary by Seniority chart.
7. **The hubs (Section 4)**: Remote vs. Local distribution.
8. **Methodology**: Brief summary of the scrapers used (Apify + GPT-4o-mini).
