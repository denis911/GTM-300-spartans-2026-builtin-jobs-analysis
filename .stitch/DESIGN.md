# GTM Landing Page Design Specs

## Aesthetic: Premium Dark Pro
- **Background**: Deep obsidian (#0a0a0b) with subtle mesh gradients (indigo/violet).
- **Typography**: Inter for body, Playfair Display or Lexend for headings.
- **Components**:
    - Glassmorphism for cards (border-opacity: 10%, backdrop-blur).
    - Accent colors: Electric Indigo (#6366f1) and Cyber Mint (#10b981).
    - Interactive Tables: Zebra striping with subtle hover highlights.
- **Layout**: Centered container, plenty of whitespace (negative space), responsive grid.

## Tech Stack (Reference)
- Vanilla CSS for styling.
- HTMX for potential data switching or modal loads.
- Semantic HTML5.
